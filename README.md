**Introduction**

This repository contains the source code, documentation, and resources for the Career Pathway Recommendation System, developed specifically for St. Clare College of Caloocan. The system leverages assessment-based decision-making to guide incoming freshmen in selecting academic programs that align with their skills, interests, and career aspirations.

By utilizing student data and machine learning algorithms, the system provides personalized course recommendations that aim to enhance career readiness and academic success.

**Objectives**

Guide Students: Provide tailored recommendations to help students choose courses aligned with their potential and career goals.
Enhance Decision-Making: Equip school administrators and guidance counselors with tools for effective student career advising.
Increase Retention and Success Rates: Align students with suitable academic programs, reducing the likelihood of program shifts or dropouts.
