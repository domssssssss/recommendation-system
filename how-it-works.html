How It Works

Take the RIASEC Test: Answer a few questions about your preferences, strengths, and work style. Explore Career Matches: Based on your results, we’ll show you careers that align with your interests and personality type. Build Your Pathway: Get recommendations for skills to develop, resources to explore, and a step-by-step roadmap to reach your career goals.

Why Take the RIASEC Test?

Our system uses the RIASEC Test to give you tailored career recommendations based on your unique personality traits. By understanding what makes you tick, we’ll connect you with careers that you’ll find both fulfilling and exciting. You’ll also receive personalized suggestions for skills to develop and courses to help you succeed in your chosen field.

Test Instruction

The test consists of 48 tasks that you will have to rate by how much you would enjoy performing each on a scale of

  1. Dislike
  2. Slightly Dislike
  3. Neutral
  4. Slightly Enjoy
  5. Enjoy
